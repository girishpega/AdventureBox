# Hardcover Readiness — Ellie Collection

**Status:** NOT READY  
**Target trim:** 8.5 × 8.5" (verify hardcover availability in KDP dashboard)  
**Page count requirement:** 75–550 pages

---

## Critical Blocker: Page Count

Hardcover requires **minimum 75 pages**. A single Ellie story (~15 pages with publishing matter) is far below threshold.

### Viable paths

| Strategy | Pages | Feasible |
|----------|-------|----------|
| Full Ellie Collection anthology (9 stories) | ~120–140 | ✅ Likely meets 75-page minimum |
| Single story hardcover | ~15 | ❌ Below minimum |
| Collection + bonus content (gallery, notes) | Variable | ⚠️ PO scope decision |

**Recommendation:** Hardcover SKU = **"The Complete Ellie Collection"** anthology only.
