# AdventureBox В· Five Premium Book Covers

Each cover should stop a parent mid-scroll. Each should look like it belongs on a nursery shelf between Disney and Donaldson.

**Format:** Portrait 2:3 ratio (standard picture book cover)  
**Resolution target:** 2400 Г— 3600px at 300 DPI  
**Style:** See [ILLUSTRATION_STYLE.md](./ILLUSTRATION_STYLE.md)  
**Title treatment:** See [TYPOGRAPHY.md](./TYPOGRAPHY.md)

---

## Cover Design Principles

1. **One hero character** dominates 60% of the cover
2. **Title readable at thumbnail size** вҖ” test at 120px width
3. **AdventureBox mark** small, bottom or top вҖ” never competes with art
4. **No plot spoilers** вҖ” sell the feeling, not the twist
5. **Each cover has a distinct dominant color** вҖ” shelf recognition at a glance

## Cover Shelf Test

When all five covers are placed side by side, they should:

| Test | Pass criteria |
|------|---------------|
| **Color variety** | Five distinct dominant hues visible at thumbnail |
| **Character recognition** | Each hero identifiable at 120px width |
| **Brand unity** | Same painterly style, same eye appeal, same warmth |
| **Age signal** | Obviously for young children, not YA, not baby board book |
| **Premium signal** | Looks purchased, not downloaded |

---

## AdventureBox Cover Mark

Small wordmark placement: bottom center or top center.  
**"AdventureBox"** in Fredoka Semibold, `#FFF9F0` on dark covers, `#2D3436` on light covers.  
Optional: tiny treasure box icon (existing brand mark) to left of wordmark.

Never larger than 8% of cover height.
