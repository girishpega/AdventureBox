# AdventureBox · Five Recurring Characters

These five characters are the permanent residents of the AdventureBox universe. They appear on book covers, sticker rewards, end screens, and future cross-over stories. Each represents a core emotional pillar of the brand.

**Design rule:** A 4-year-old should recognize each character from silhouette alone.

---

## 1. Pip — *The Light*

**Role in brand:** Bedtime, guidance, the gentle glow that leads you home.

### Visual Design

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Body** | Tiny round glow — roughly the size of a child's fist. Soft golden orb core with translucent amber wings. |
| **Wings** | Delicate, four petal-shaped wings like a flower. Semi-transparent, catch light beautifully. |
| **Eyes** | Large, dark, gentle — 40% of face. Always a white catchlight. Sleepy-happy default expression. |
| **Lantern** | Optional prop — a miniature warm lantern on a thin stem, carried when guiding. |
| **Color** | Core `#FDCB6E`, glow `#FFEAA7`, wing tint `#A29BFE` at edges |
| **Silhouette test** | Round body + four wing points + one glowing dot |

### Personality

Curious, calm, brave in a quiet way. Pip never shouts. Pip whispers secrets about stars. The friend who sits on your pillow and says *"Ready?"*

### Story domains

Night skies, stars, dreams, gentle magic, lullabies.

### Existing story

⭐ *Pip and the Sleepy Star Parade*

### Character prompt (for AI consistency)

```
Pip, a tiny golden firefly character for a premium children's brand. Round 
glowing amber body (#FDCB6E), four delicate translucent petal-shaped wings, 
large gentle dark eyes with white catchlights, soft warm halo of light around 
body. Expression: calm wonder. Pixar-level appeal. Painterly watercolor style. 
No harsh outlines. Approximately 8cm tall in-world scale.
```

## 2. Ellie — *The Heart*

**Role in brand:** Kindness, friendship, gentle strength. The big friend with a soft touch.

### Visual Design

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Body** | Baby elephant calf — round, slightly clumsy, big head. No tusks. |
| **Color** | Warm gray-violet `#B2BEC3`, highlights `#DFE6E9`, shadows `#636E72` |
| **Ears** | Oversized, floppy, pink inner edge `#FAB1A0` |
| **Eyes** | Amber-brown `#6D4C41`, long lashes, always kind |
| **Trunk** | Short, stubby, highly expressive — the primary emotion tool |
| **Silhouette test** | Round body + big ears + trunk curve |

### Personality

Tender, loyal, sometimes silly. Ellie feels deeply. Laughs with her whole body. The friend who kneels down to your level.

### Story domains

Friendship, animals, helping, meadow adventures, emotional warmth.

### Existing story

🐘 *Ellie and the Tiny Lost Duck*

### Character prompt

```
Ellie, a baby elephant calf character for a premium children's brand. Round 
soft gray-violet body (#B2BEC3), oversized floppy ears with pink inner edges, 
big amber-brown eyes with long lashes and white catchlights, short expressive 
trunk, no tusks. Gentle, kind expression. Pixar-level appeal. Painterly 
watercolor style. Child-sized proportions — big head, small body.
```

## 3. Finn — *The Play*

**Role in brand:** Joy, silliness, movement. The splash that makes you giggle.

### Visual Design

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Body** | River otter pup — sleek but fluffy, approximately 18 inches tall standing |
| **Color** | Rich brown `#8D6E63` body, cream belly `#FFF9F0`, whiskers white |
| **Eyes** | Bright hazel `#6D4C41`, always slightly mischievous |

## 5. Sage — *The Storyteller*

**Role in brand:** Reading, cozy corners, the love of books. The friend who always has one more story.

### Visual Design

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Body** | Owl chick — fluffy, round, not yet fully grown (no sharp adult owl look) |

Cozy, patient, loves the sound of pages turning. Sage doesn't rush. Sage creates the space where stories happen. The friend who says *"Once more?"*

### Story domains

## Ensemble Rules

When multiple characters appear together (covers, marketing, sticker sheets):

1. **Pip floats** — always highest in frame, glowing softly
2. **Ellie is center** — anchor of the group, largest presence
3. **Finn is moving** — never static, mid-action
4. **Nova looks outward** — drawing the eye to the adventure
5. **Sage is grounded** — lowest, seated, book in lap

Maximum three characters per cover unless it's a special "AdventureBox Friends" edition.

---

## Character Do-Nots

- Never scary expressions or sharp teeth
- Never human children as main characters (parents appear only as hands/shadows if ever)
- Never brand mascots breaking the fourth wall with UI language
- Never inconsistent proportions between stories
- Never adult versions of these characters (they stay young forever)
